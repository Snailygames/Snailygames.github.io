About Us

We are a Face Book Alternative to express your true self

Ahoy-Matey is Social Media site, owned and operated by the Delivery Ahoy company. Our goal is to give people a non invasive social media, uncluttered by excessive surveillance, and interference. All members MUST abide by the laws of their country, AND the Charter of Rights and Freedoms of the Canadian Constitution. Your Circle will be limited to 160 people, and we do not allow Groups in excess of a thousand members. You will be required to use your legal name, and not a pseudonym; this is to help prevent Bots from phishing your information. External links to other sites must come from a short list of sites that are vetted for journalistic integrity and truth on all sides of the political spectrum. Unlike Face Book, Ahoy-Matey embraces diverse views, as long as they are not Hate Speech, or patently false. Welcome to Ahoy-Matey, where everyone is a Pirate, and our goal is to bring the world on deck... Or if you are a problem; walk the plank! Join now and enjoy our free social media site.
